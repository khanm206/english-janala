Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Our website is free for all users. You can access all lessons and vocabulary without any charge.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional. You can use the website and view all content without signing up.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by exploring our lessons regularly, learning new words, and practicing them in context.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
At the moment, we focus on helping you learn and practice vocabulary. Certificates are not provided, but completing lessons will improve your skills!
